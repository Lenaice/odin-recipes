Fried Rice Recipe

Fried rice is a flavorful and colorful dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, protein, and seasonings.
It is a popular meal in many cultures, known for its versatility and delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, sweet corn)
  • 1/2 cup diced chicken, shrimp, or beef (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  3. If using, add the diced chicken, shrimp, or beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice and curry powder, stirring well to combine.
  6. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
